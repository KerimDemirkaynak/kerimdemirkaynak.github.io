About Me

I am a content creator based in Düzce. I focus on producing content about anime and manga, while also pursuing my interests in technology and software development. In addition, I volunteer as a reviewer on the Turkish version of Wikipedia.

My Work

I create subtitles for a variety of animated series from both Asia and the West. I have also designed social media graphics and customizable templates for other content creators.

I produce videos for YouTube, creating animated intros and other video projects using professional software like After Effects.

Since 2022, I have been developing a visual novel project called Turkish Ice-Cream using the Ren'Py game engine.

Since 2017, I have been writing blog posts about animation and technology, as well as designing websites and releasing them as open-source projects.
